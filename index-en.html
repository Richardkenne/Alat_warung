FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes, 100% free. Track debts, stock, and finances without paying anything. Pro is optional if you want cloud sync.

Do I need to download an app?

Nope. Open alatwarung.id in your phone browser (Chrome, Safari) and start using it. You can also save it to your home screen.

Where is my data stored?

Free version: on your phone (browser localStorage). Pro version: securely in the cloud, accessible from any device.

Does it auto-send WhatsApp messages?

No. We only create the message template. You copy and send it yourself. You're always in control.

How is this different from BukuWarung or CrediBook?

Alat Warung is built to be as lightweight as possible — no download, no login, works right in your browser. Plus we have AI WhatsApp message generation that other apps don't offer.
